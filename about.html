At Lifestyle, we are dedicated to providing top-notch traditional grooming services that blend timeless techniques with modern flair. Our team of skilled professionals is passionate about helping you look and feel your best.

Founded in 2022 in SOWETO, Lifestyle has grown from a small local barbershop to a renowned grooming destination. Our commitment to quality, customer satisfaction, and community engagement has been the cornerstone of our success.

We believe that grooming is not just about appearance; it's about confidence, self-care, Established in 2022, The Lifestyle Barber is a local barbershop located in Soweto. It has built a loyal clientele by specialising in classic clean cuts, razor shave, and beard grooming.
